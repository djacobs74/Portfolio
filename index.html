About

Hi, I’m David Jacobs. I’m a fullstack developer based in Southern California.

I am an Army veteran and former telecommunications technician. I have always enjoyed both the hardware and software aspects of technology, and the challenges that come with both. In April of 2016, I decided to quit my technician job and make the leap into the world of software engineering. I attended the Firehose Project bootcamp, where I made my first applications. Since then, I have continued to learn and grow as a developer, creating increasingly complex and challenging applications and features.
